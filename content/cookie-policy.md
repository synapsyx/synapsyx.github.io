# Cookie Policy
_Last updated: 28 April 2026_

This page explains the cookies and browser storage used by synapsyx.com and complements our [Privacy Policy](/privacy-policy/).

---

## 1  Summary

This site does **not** use analytics, tracking, or marketing cookies. We do not run advertising, social-tracking pixels, or third-party measurement scripts. The site has no forms, log-in, newsletter, or user-upload features.

Only two categories of data are stored in your browser when you visit:

- **Essential hosting cookies** placed by our hosting provider GitHub Pages and its CDN Fastly, used solely for technical operation, load balancing, and rate-limiting.
- **A single functional `localStorage` entry** that this site uses to remember your light / dark theme preference.

Because these are strictly necessary or strictly user-initiated, no consent banner is shown.

| Name | Source | Purpose | Type | Retention |
|------|--------|---------|------|-----------|
| `_gh_sess` | GitHub Inc. / B.V. | Session handling for the hosting infrastructure | Essential | Session |
| `_octo` | GitHub Inc. / B.V. | Internal request and load metrics for GitHub | Essential | Per cookie expiration |
| `color_mode` | GitHub Inc. / B.V. | GitHub-side theme handling | Essential | Per cookie expiration |
| Cookies set by Fastly | Fastly Inc. (CDN) | Routing, load balancing, rate-limiting | Essential | Session or short-lived |

These cookies are required for the site to be served and protected against abuse. They are set by the hosting infrastructure, not by synapsyx.

**Legal basis:** legitimate interest under Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR (technical operation and security of the website).

---

## 3  Browser storage used by synapsyx.com

The site itself uses the browser's `localStorage` for one purpose only:

| Key | Purpose | Type | Retention |
|-----|---------|------|-----------|
| `synx_v2_theme` | Remembers whether you have switched the site to light or dark mode | Functional | Persists in your browser until you clear it |

This entry is created only after you actively click the theme toggle in the navigation. It contains the literal value `light` or `dark` and nothing else. It is never transmitted to a server — it stays in your browser.

**Legal basis:** legitimate interest under Art. 6 (1)(f) GDPR (preserving a user-initiated display preference).

---

## 4  Third-party cookies

We do not embed third-party content on this site (no YouTube, Vimeo, social-media widgets, comment systems, maps, or chat tools) and we do not load third-party scripts. As a result, no third-party cookies are set when you visit.

---

## 5  Managing cookies and storage

You can clear or block cookies and `localStorage` through your browser settings at any time. Doing so will not affect access to this site beyond resetting your saved theme preference.

Common entry points:

- **Chrome / Edge:** Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Settings → Privacy → Manage Website Data
