01 · About

A Vienna-based team building proprietary AI, not wrappers.

synapsyx builds AI products that live inside institutional workflows — cross-border project teams, university career offices, EU consortia — where the work is specific, the standards are high, and generic assistants fall short.

2024 Founded · Vienna, AT

We build proprietary AI products for institutional project teams, universities, and education consortia. Our practice is grounded in context engineering, evals, and iteration on live user feedback — not prompt tricks, not off-the-shelf RAG.

The team combines computer science, engineering, economics, and law, with hands-on experience shipping agentic systems into environments where accuracy, citation, and human review are non-negotiable.

We work in English and German, ship into EU and international programmes, and prefer small focused products over broad platforms. Our two current products — G/S-Flow and ePATH — are both in active production use.
